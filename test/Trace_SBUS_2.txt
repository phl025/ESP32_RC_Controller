*
* Serial 2 started at 100000 baud rate, 8E2, Inverted
*
* Code :
* 	Serial2.begin(100000, SERIAL_8E2, 36, -1, true);
*

* References :
https://uwarg-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/ZP/pages/2238283817/SBUS+Protocol
https://github.com/bolderflight/sbus/blob/main/README.md
The SBUS protocol uses an inverted serial logic with a baud rate of 100000, 8 data bits, even parity, and 2 stop bits. The SBUS packet is 25 bytes long consisting of:
    Byte[0]: SBUS header, (0x0F)
    Byte[1 -22]: 16 servo channels, 11 bits each
    Byte[23]
        Bit 0: channel 17 (0x01)
        Bit 1: channel 18 (0x02)
        Bit 2: frame lost (0x04)
        Bit 3: failsafe activated (0x08)
    Byte[24]: SBUS footer, (0x00)

0F DC 03 9F FA C0 C7 0A F0 81 0F 7C E0 03 1F F8 58 A1 3E EF 91 4F 76 00 00 
	[header] 0F
	[data]	 DC 03 9F FA C0 C7 0A F0 81 0F 7C E0 03 1F F8 58 A1 3E EF 91 4F 76
	[footer] 00 00
